My Projects

MedMap

MedMap is a GIS-powered, AI-driven emergency healthcare platform that connects patients, ambulances, and hospitals in real-time. It provides live tracking of hospital bed availability, ventilators, and emergency resources, reducing critical response time by 30-50%. Using AI-driven ambulance navigation, it optimizes routes, ensuring faster medical assistance. MedMap integrates with government emergency services, blood banks, and pharmacies, offering one-click access to life-saving care. Designed for urban and rural scalability, it enhances healthcare infrastructure, supports disaster management, and aligns with Smart Cities & Digital India initiatives. MedMap saves lives by making emergency healthcare accessible, efficient, and data-driven.

Integrated Railway Intelligence System

The Integrated Railway Intelligence System (IRIS)is an advanced AI-powered software that automates route scheduling and train operations by integrating multiple railway management systems. Using a"Reinforcement Learning model", IRIS unifies the Train Management System (TMS), Freight Operations Intelligence System (FOIS), National Train Enquiry System (NTES),and Passenger Reservation System (PRS) into a single intelligent platform. This system enhances real-time train tracking, optimizes freight logistics, and improves passenger ticketing while ensuring efficient train movement. By leveraging predictive analytics and adaptive decision-making, IRIS minimizes delays, optimizes resource utilization, and enhances the overall efficiency of railway operations.
